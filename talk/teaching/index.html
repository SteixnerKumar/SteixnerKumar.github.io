Teaching at Hamburg University

Saurabh Steixner-Kumar
Follow

Abstract

In this seminar topics such as perception, attention, consciousness, reasoning & problem solving, judgement & decision making, language, etc. will be covered and deepened. Based on original literature, a more in-depth understanding for selected topics that go beyond the contents of the lecture will be elaborated. Thereto, theories and findings will be presented and discussed and applied to practical contexts. With the help of current literature, we will delve into adjacent areas such as neuropsychology and human computer/robotic interaction. The goal is that students learn to read and understand original literature so that they can critically evaluate scientific methods and theories. At the same time train their scientific thinking and presentation techniques.
